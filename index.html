Need To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in begins at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out may be arranged based on availability, complimentary for suite guests.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Yes, all bookings include a gourmet breakfast at Le Astoria, featuring both a la carte and buffet selections crafted by our executive chef.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Absolutely. Our chauffeured luxury vehicle service is available for airport pick-up and drop-off. Please arrange this at least 24 hours prior to arrival via our concierge.

Is the hotel pet-friendly?

We welcome well-behaved pets under 25 lbs in select rooms with prior notice. A pet amenity package is available upon request.

What is your cancellation policy?

Reservations may be cancelled free of charge up to 48 hours before arrival. Cancellations within 48 hours are subject to a one-night charge.
